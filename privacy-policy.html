Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Introduction

Prompter Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and related services.

By using Prompter Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Data Collection - We Collect Nothing

Prompter Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything happens locally on your computer.

What Stays on Your Device

  • API Keys: Your API keys are stored locally in your browser extension
  • Extension Settings: Your preferences are saved locally on your device
  • Prompt Text: All text processing happens locally on your computer

What We Never Access

  • No Data Collection: We don't collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics
  • No Server Storage: We don't have servers that store your information
  • No Tracking: We don't track your browsing activity or extension usage
  • No File Access: We don't access or read files on your device

How Prompter Pro Works

Since we don't collect any data, here's how Prompter Pro actually works:

  • Local Processing: All prompt enhancement happens locally on your computer
  • Direct API Calls: When you enhance prompts, the extension makes direct calls to AI providers using your API keys
  • No Data Transmission: We don't see, store, or process your prompts on our servers
  • Privacy by Design: The extension is built to work entirely offline except for the AI API calls you initiate

Data Processing - 100% Local

Complete Local Processing

All data processing happens on your computer. We don't have any servers or cloud infrastructure that processes your data.

AI Provider Integration

When you enhance prompts, the extension makes direct API calls to AI providers using your API keys:

  • Direct Connection: Your browser extension connects directly to AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.)
  • Your API Keys: All API calls use your personal API credentials
  • No Intermediary: We don't act as a middleman - your data goes directly to the AI provider
  • Provider Privacy: Your data is subject to the privacy policies of the AI providers you choose to use

Data Security - Not Applicable

Since we don't collect or store any data, traditional data security measures don't apply. However, we ensure:

  • Local Storage Security: Your API keys and settings are stored securely in your browser's local storage
  • No Data Exposure: We can't expose your data because we don't have access to it
  • Extension Security: The extension code is designed with privacy and security in mind
  • Direct API Security: When you use AI providers, you benefit from their security measures

AI Provider Integration

Prompter Pro can work with various AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) through your API keys:

  • Direct Integration: Your extension connects directly to AI providers using your API keys
  • No Data Sharing: We don't share your data because we don't have access to it
  • Provider Policies: Your interactions with AI platforms are governed by their respective privacy policies
  • Your Choice: You choose which AI providers to use and configure with your own API keys

Data Retention - Nothing to Retain

We don't retain any data because we don't collect any data. Here's what happens locally:

  • Prompt Text: Processed locally and never stored anywhere
  • API Keys: Stored locally in your browser; you control when to remove them
  • Extension Settings: Stored locally on your device; you can reset anytime
  • No Server Storage: We have no servers to store data on

Your Complete Control

You have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device. You can:

  • View Everything: All data is stored locally in your browser extension - you can see it all
  • Delete Anytime: Remove API keys and reset extension settings whenever you want
  • Full Control: Manage all extension settings and API key preferences locally
  • Complete Privacy: Your data never leaves your computer unless you choose to use AI providers
  • Uninstall Anytime: Remove the extension and all local data disappears

Children's Privacy

Prompter Pro is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal information, there's no risk of collecting data from children. All data remains on the user's device.

International Data Transfers - Not Applicable

We don't transfer any data internationally because we don't collect or store any data. If you choose to use AI providers, any data transfers are between you and those providers directly, not through us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice through the extension when significant changes occur

Your continued use of Prompter Pro after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

GDPR Compliance - Not Applicable

GDPR requirements don't apply to Prompter Pro because we don't process any personal data. Since all data remains on your device and we don't collect, store, or process any personal information, GDPR data processing rules are not relevant to our service.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA) - Not Applicable

CCPA requirements don't apply to Prompter Pro because we don't collect, use, share, or sell any personal information. Since all data remains on your device and we don't have access to any personal information, California privacy rights regarding data collection are not relevant to our service.
