Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about using Prompter Pro

Where do I paste my API keys?

Open the extension’s settings page and add your API keys for the models you use (e.g., OpenAI, Claude, Gemini). Keys are stored locally on your device.

Which sites does Prompter Pro work on?

Anywhere you write prompts: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity and more. It also helps in docs, email, and CMS editors.

Does my text leave my computer?

We don’t collect or store your content. The extension runs locally and only communicates with providers you configure via your API keys.

How does the AI enhancement work?

Prompter Pro uses advanced AI models to analyze your input and automatically enhance it with better structure, clarity, and detail. It adds context, improves grammar, and makes your prompts more effective for AI interactions.

Is Prompter Pro free to use?

Yes! Prompter Pro is completely free to use. You only need to provide your own API keys for the AI models you want to use. The extension itself doesn't charge any fees.
